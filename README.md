# 🐚 Lanii

> *Your emotional journey, one seashell at a time.*

Lanii is a mobile app for iOS that helps you register and reflect on your emotions in a gentle, immersive way — guided by Hermie, your friendly hermit crab companion.

---

## 🦀 Meet Hermie

Every session begins with you waking up Hermie, your loyal companion through this emotional journey. Once awake, Hermie guides you across the island — a world where every landscape reflects a piece of how you feel.

---

## 🗺️ The Island of Emotions

At the heart of Lanii is an interactive map of a personal island, where each location represents a different emotional world:

| Location | Emotion |
|---|---|
| 🏖️ Beach of Joy | Happiness & excitement |
| 🌊 Lake of Sadness | Melancholy & grief |
| 🌋 Volcano of Anger | Frustration & rage |
| 🌲 Forest of Fear | Anxiety & worry |
| 🕳️ Cave of Disgust | Discomfort & aversion |
| 🌸 Garden of Surprise | Wonder & the unexpected |

Place your cursor on the map to choose where you feel drawn — and let the island lead you deeper.

---

## 🐚 Collecting Seashells

Once you've chosen your emotional territory, you'll arrive at a seashore scattered with seashells. Each shell holds a different emotion waiting to be uncovered. Select the one that resonates with you, then:

- ✍️ **Write a note** to capture your thoughts in the moment
- 🎙️ **Record your voice** to preserve how you truly sounded and felt

Your seashells are saved to your personal collection — a private archive of your emotional journey over time.

---

## 👂 Your Seashell Collection

Visit your collection anytime to revisit past emotions. Hold your phone up to your ear to listen to your audio recordings — just like pressing a seashell to your ear to hear the sound of the ocean.
